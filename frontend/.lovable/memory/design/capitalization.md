# Capitalization rules

- **Page H1 titles & sidebar nav items**: Title Case
  ("Menu & Recipes", "Suppliers", "Forecasting")
- **Section headings (h2/h3)**: sentence case
  ("Variance report", "Recent purchase orders", "Quick count complete")
- **Buttons & CTAs**: sentence case
  ("New purchase order", "Add supplier", "Save draft", "Create recipe")
- **Table column headers**: sentence case
  ("Unit cost", "Menu price", "Food cost")
- **Status pills / one-word labels**: Title Case
  ("Active", "Sent", "Draft", "Critical", "High", "Accepted")
- **Tab triggers**: sentence case
  ("Current stock", "Quick count", "Full count")
